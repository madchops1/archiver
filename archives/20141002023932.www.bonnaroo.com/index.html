SuperJam 2013: R. Kelly sings Sam Cooke

The Bonnaroo SuperJam is known for 'over the top' surprises. When R. Kelly took the stage at 1:30AM the audience responded with a collective gasp before erupting in celebration. This was uncharted territory for both the band members and R. Kelly as they had never met one another before, let alone played music together in front of a packed crowd.
